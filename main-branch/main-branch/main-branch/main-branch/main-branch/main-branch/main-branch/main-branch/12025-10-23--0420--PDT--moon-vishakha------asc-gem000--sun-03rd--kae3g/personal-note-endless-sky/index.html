🎬 The Jodo Trilogy (1 2 3)

The Jodo trilogy represents a progression through three states of being, each building upon the last in an endless cycle of growth and understanding.

Jodo 1: The Beginning

Where it all starts. The first step into the endless sky. Like a grain sprouting from the earth, reaching toward the infinite.

Jodo 2: The Journey

The middle path, where wisdom grows. The grain becomes a stalk, reaching higher into the endless sky, learning from the wind and rain.

Jodo 3: The Return

Coming full circle, but changed. The grain has grown, learned, and now returns to share its wisdom with the next generation.

🌾 The Eastwood Philosophy: Bad → Better → Least-Worst

Bad: The starting point. Raw, unrefined, full of potential but lacking direction. Like a grain before it knows its purpose.

Better: The journey of improvement. Learning, growing, making mistakes and learning from them. The grain begins to understand its place in the endless sky.

Least-Worst: The wisdom of acceptance. Not perfect, but the best possible outcome given the circumstances. The grain has grown as tall as it can, reaching toward the endless sky with all its might.

🌱 Bad → 🌿 Better → 🌾 Least-Worst ↓ ↓ ↓ Raw Growing Wisdom Potential → Learning → Acceptance
