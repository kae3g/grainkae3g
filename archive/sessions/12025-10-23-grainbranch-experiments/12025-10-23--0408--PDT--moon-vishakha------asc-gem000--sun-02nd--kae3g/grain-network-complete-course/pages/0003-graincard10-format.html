📐 The Perfect Portrait

Graincard10 is a standardized knowledge format optimized for both human reading and machine processing. Like a playing card, business card, or index card - but for the entire knowledge base.

Width: 80 characters
Height: 110 lines
Aspect: Portrait (9:16-ish)
Capacity: 10,000 pages (0000-9999)

🌾 Why These Dimensions?

80 Characters:

  • Historical standard from punch cards and terminals
  • Optimal line length for reading comprehension
  • Works in all text editors and terminals
  • Forces conciseness and clarity

110 Lines:

  • Enough depth for complete concepts
  • Not too long to lose reader attention
  • Fits well in modern displays
  • Printable on standard paper (portrait)

10,000 Pages (0000-9999):

  • Enough for comprehensive course material
  • Sortable with zero-padded numbering
  • Modular: each page is self-contained unit
  • Scalable: link pages in any order

Content Types per Graincard

📖 Educational: Lessons, tutorials, explanations
📋 Reference: API docs, specs, standards
🎨 Visual: ASCII art, diagrams, illustrations
💭 Philosophical: Reflections, wisdom, guidance
🔧 Technical: Code examples, configurations
🌟 Inspirational: Stories, quotes, motivation

The graincard10 format provides structure without stifling creativity. Constraints enable focus, focus enables depth.
